S.T.A.L.K.E.R Shadow of Chernobyl

In 2006, a second disaster occurred that changed the area around the Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant. The Zone became home to mutants, anomalies, and artifacts. Stalkers-lone individuals who risk their lives to venture into the Zone in search of wealth and fame-are the ones who explore it. You are one of them. Uncover the secrets of the Zone and learn the truth about the disaster.

Game Features

  • Unique atmosphere of the Zone
  • Realistic ballistics
  • Dangerous anomalies
  • Valuable artifacts
  • Non-linear storyline

Survival

Radiation, hunger, and dangerous mutants are your main enemies in the Zone

Exploration

Explore abandoned locations and discover the secrets of the Zone

Artifacts

Search for rare artifacts in dangerous anomalous fields
